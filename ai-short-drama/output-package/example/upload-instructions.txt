Manual upload checklist
1. Confirm final.mp4 hash matches qc-report.json/master record.
2. Confirm the approved title, description, hashtags, cover, visibility, and schedule.
3. Confirm the platform AI/adapted-content declaration.
4. Upload through the platform's official UI or authorized API.
5. Record the returned platform work ID and URL in the publication task.

This example does not authorize browser automation or a real upload.
